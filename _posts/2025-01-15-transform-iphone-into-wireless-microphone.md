## Getting Started: Tips for Best Results

### 1. Choose the Right Speaker

- **For streaming**: Use a Bluetooth speaker with good frequency response
- **For gaming**: Low-latency Bluetooth headphones work best
- **For karaoke**: Home stereo systems provide the best sound
- **For professional use**: Wired connections offer the most reliability

### 2. Optimize Your Environment

- **Reduce background noise**: Use in quiet spaces when possible
- **Position your phone**: Hold it 6-12 inches from your mouth
- **Test your setup**: Do a quick test before important sessions
- **Check battery**: Ensure your phone has sufficient charge

### 3. Experiment with Effects

- **Start subtle**: Begin with light echo or reverb
- **Test in real-time**: Adjust while speaking to hear changes
- **Save presets**: If your app supports it, save your favorite settings
- **Match your use case**: Different effects work better for different purposes

### 4. Manage Your Recordings

- **Regular cleanup**: Delete unwanted recordings to save space
- **Cloud backup**: Enable automatic cloud sync for important recordings
- **Organize by project**: Use descriptive names when exporting
- **Share efficiently**: Use cloud links for large files
